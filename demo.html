Trained on 7 modules, evaluated on 3 it never saw. Held-out mean 0.38 → 0.77; binary_search 0.39 → 1.00 without ever training on it — a transferable test-writing skill, not memorization. roman_to_int only reached 0.51 (partial transfer) and is_balanced barely moved (already high) — shown honestly.

The reward is non-gameable: it's mutation-kill rate scored by the environment, no LLM judge — a no-op / assert-False suite scores 0. Eval is n=5 per module (temp 0.7), so individual numbers carry ~±0.1 noise; the direction is unambiguous.

Separate live-baseline context: a frontier model (Qwen3-8B) scored 0.90 through this same environment — the gym discriminates skill, and the 3B model's training curve climbed from a first-10-step mean of 0.13 to a last-10-step mean of 0.82 over 80 steps.
